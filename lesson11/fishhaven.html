Fire in Nounan destroys barn

The following was received from Bear Lake County Fire:. On March 16th 2020 at 07:36, The Bear Lake County Fire Department along with the Caribou County Fire Department responded to the report of a fire at 15901 Nounan, Road in Nounan. When the first fire crews arrived on the scene the shop/garage was reported to be a fully involved structure. Multiple units responded to the scene and the fire was extinguished and mop up was performed. Fire crews continued to perform salvage and investigation activates for some time after that.
