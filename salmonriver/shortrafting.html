Short Rafting Experiences

Idaho's Salmon River is is between the Frank Church River and the No Return Wilderness, the largest wilderness in the lower 48 states. The Salmon River is one of the largest rivers in the United States with the second deepest canyon; the first being the Grand Canyon. This river spans over 85 miles of remote wilderness allowing one to connect with nature and disconnect from our digital life.

Salmon River is characterized by its rollercoaster rapids set between calm peacefull waters with deep green pools and expansive canyon walls. The banks of the rivers have sandy beaches for taking a break along with permitted overnight camping. Come experience the Salmon River with us today!

3 Hour Experience - $45 per person


Take in the beauty of the Salmon River in an 3 hour experience you will never forget. The 3 Hour trip is a great beginner course if you have never experienced white water rafting before.

-First hour will be experiencing mild to moderate rapids.
-Second hour is spent exploring the sandy beaches and expansive canyon walls.
-Third hour will explore the rivers natural wildlife and experience a few more mild rapids.

No Experience Necessary.

Included in Trip:


- Transportation from Office to River
- All rafting gear included (life jacket, raft, paddles, helmet)
- Transportation back to our Office.

Things to bring:

- Swimsuit or clothes that can get wet.
- Waterproof Camera
- Sunscreen
- Sunglasses
- Water Shoes
