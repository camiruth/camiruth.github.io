Site Purpose

Weather. Sunny, Rain, Wind, Snow and everything in between. We let you know what to plan on and how to prepare. Our site is virtually about how to prepare for what the day ahead brings. It will let users know how to dress so they don't accidently forget a jacket.

Also, our slogan is "never miss a sunny day again" and the reason behind that, is our website will tell the client when its going to be a good day to play outside. Thus you can plan your week around the best days.

This is no ordinary weather site. It is all about creating the best day possible for our users. There will be a 7 day forcast that will show high and lows, wind speeds, precipitation, how to dress for the day, what it feels like outside, and how sunny it will be.

Users will never question the weather again as they will know what is coming their way.
